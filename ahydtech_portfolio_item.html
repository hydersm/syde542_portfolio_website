Ahydtech Website

Quick Description

Ahydtech Geomorphic is a Canadian company created by my father, Bahar SM, and located in Guelph, Ontario. Ahydtech provides engineering consulting services in field management, numerical modeling, coastal engineering, municipal environment assessment, hydrologic modeling and hydraulic modeling.

During the initial phases of the company, my father created a basic website to help advertise his new firm. However, he quickly found out that the website was poorly designed, and as a result it did not attract many customers. So he asked me to redesign the website for him. Thus, this page describes the design and implementation process of the new website for Ahydtech.

User Requirements

Interview

To gather requirements, I first interviewed the site owner. The questions below were asked. The answer was recorded in point form.

  • What is Ahydtech?
    • Ahydtech Geomorphic is a Canadian company located in Guelph, Ontario
    • We provide services in field measurement, hydrologic and hydraulic modeling, numerical modeling, coastal engineering and municipal environmental Assessment
  • What is the purpose of the website?
    • A place where people can find out about the company (goals, objectives and services)
    • Advertise the services and products offered by the company
  • Who are the users?
    • Public offices (municipalities)
    • Conservation authorities
    • Provincial and federal governments
    • Private land owner and land developers
  • What are the user’s needs? Limitations? Preferences?
    • They want to see whether the company is capable of doing the job (they look at experience, staff, qualifications)
  • What are the users’ tasks? Which are important?
    • Navigation (very important)
    • Read information (ease of finding and reading information is important)
    • Easy way for customers to contact company staff / purchase services (this is not very important at the moment, just showing contact information is enough)
