FYDP Enterprise Dashboard Web Application

Quick Description

For my fourth year design project, I formed a group with Adi Pruthi, Martin Fung and Arya Talebzadeh. We decided that we would work on a product in the advertisement industry. Our goal was to put static physical ads on the same page, figuratively, as online ads. An ideal solution created will aim to mimic a model used for online ads by learning from characteristics of its viewers, serving well targeted digital ads to them and reporting useful metrics back to the enterprises.

Our project consisted of 3 components: a demographic recognition system, an ad display system, and an enterprise dashboard. This page focuses on the design and implementation of the ad dashboard. This dashboard will be reponsible for creating ads, managing ads, and showing ad statistics.
